Game overview

The Piggy Experience

In this game, players take on the role of business pigs doing what they do best, chasing fortune. In their world, they compete to inflate assets, mislead opponents, and exploit every opportunity to grow richer than everyone else at the table.

[IMAGE: Setting & Atmosphere]
Setting & Atmosphere

A World of Greed

High above the city, inside an absurdly opulent bank vault stuffed with gold, marble pillars, and over-the-top ornamentation, a group of sharply dressed pig bankers scramble to hoard as much wealth as possible. In Greedy Piggies, players embody these gluttonous financiers, chasing riches in a world that reflects their fortunes. As their wealth grows, the vault becomes increasingly extravagant and dazzling—but when greed leads to ruin, the cracks begin to show. Flickering lights, fractured walls, and panicked piggy animations signal the slow collapse of their once-glittering empire.
